What Do We Do

Climate Change is occuring, and humans have always been at blame. I simply presume that this is our most severe environmental issue. This page was intended to remind everyone that we might loss this world if we are not going to all work together to combat climate change. We may not see its impact today, tomorrow, or even the next month, but we must think about it for the next generation's future. As a first year college student pursuing Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, it gives me a great pleasure to use my knowledge in this kind of situation because I am not just fighting for my own future but for the future of everyone.
