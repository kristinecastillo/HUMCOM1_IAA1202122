The Golden Trio

Daniel Radcliffe - Harry Potter
Emma Watson - Hermione Granger
Rupert Grint - Ron Weasly

The Golden Trio, which was the most well known trio in the Harry Potter series. This trio includes our favorite main characters, intelligent Hermione Granger, loyal Ron Weasley, and courageous Harry Potter. In the course of a few years, Harry Potter fans have decided to create new trios. Like the silver trio, and the bronze trio! The golden trio was the name given to Harry, Ron, and Hermione. Today we know them as mostly the main important trio of the series. Since we mostly follow their lives and their journeys from their first year to last up to fighting together in the Second Wizarding War that took place in 1998.

Sypnosis

The story of Harry Potter begins as he is about to celebrate his eleventh birthday. Up until this time, Harry's birthdays have come and gone like any other day; they have been nothing worth celebrating. You see, Harry has lived in a cupboard under the stairs in his Uncle Vernon's and Aunt Petunia's house. He has never received a birthday present worth remembering. Harry's life changes in an instant. The letter he receives is an acceptance and invitation to study at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
